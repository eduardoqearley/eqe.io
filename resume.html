Who I Am

Technology executive with 25+ years whose career has one through-line: building the platform infrastructure that removes the gatekeeping layer — taking what was reserved for a few and making it available to everyone at scale.

At WMS, I built the platform that moved casino entertainment off the physical floor and online, creating a $30M category that didn’t previously exist. At SpotHero, I rebuilt the infrastructure that gave any driver access to parking information previously locked in tribal city knowledge — 100x delivery capacity, 10,000x transactional throughput. At Otus, I built the data platform that gave K–12 schools enterprise-grade analytics they couldn’t otherwise afford. At AWS, I owned the tooling — EKS Addons, eksctl, ACK — that made Kubernetes accessible to teams without dedicated platform engineering. At Meta, I’m running AI-native transformation of capacity governance infrastructure that supports billions in annual ad revenue on a platform used by billions of people.

I own the full stack: technical architecture, product direction, and the org that executes it. PM-less product ownership is the model — I have been the product function and the engineering function simultaneously at Meta, Otus, and WMS. That’s not a footnote; it’s the operating model. AI-native engineering is a first-class practice: AI agents in infrastructure operations, AI-assisted management workflows, declarative AI IaC. Small teams, bold bets, first to market, light process. Looking for the next platform to scale: CTO/CPO at a company whose mission is access.
