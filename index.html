Technology & Product Executive — Seattle & Chicago

Technology executive with 25+ years whose career has one through-line: building the platform infrastructure that removes the gatekeeping layer — taking what was reserved for a few and making it available to everyone at scale.

At WMS, I built the platform that moved casino entertainment off the physical floor and online, creating a $30M category that didn’t previously exist. At SpotHero, I rebuilt the infrastructure that gave any driver the parking information previously locked in tribal city knowledge — 100x delivery capacity, 10,000x throughput. At Otus, enterprise-grade analytics for K–12 schools. At AWS, the tooling that made Kubernetes accessible at scale. At Meta, AI-native transformation of capacity governance infrastructure supporting billions in annual ad revenue.

I own the full stack: technical architecture, product direction, and the org that executes it. PM-less product ownership is the model. Small teams, bold bets, light process. Looking for the next platform to scale: CTO/CPO at a company whose mission is access.
